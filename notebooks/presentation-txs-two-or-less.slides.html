Things we are interested in¶

  • Payments over Bitcoin – dynamics of user and wallet behavior
  • Fees – a good statistical model, and general behavior description
  • Structural changes of wallet behavior
  • Privacy – anything we can find, anyone else can (fix!)

How we are doing this¶

  • Read from Google BigQuery Bitcoin public datasets
  • Run our DataFlow pipeline to process and identify transactions
  • Publish as a BigQuery table for analysis
  • https://github.com/josibake/bitcoin-data-pipelines

How we are doing this¶

transactions that are believed to be payments¶

  • Transactions with two or less outputs (TX2OL)
  • Considered most likely
  • Does this transaction set have enough volume and economic value to be worth studying?
  • Yes, about 80% of value being transacted from 2010 - 2021 (next slide - yes with a graph)

USD value of transactions¶

  • Excluding single output and nonstandard, we consider the mean and median USD value over time
  • Payment value, this seems to fall with in a reasonable range for a "retail payment."
  • Change values tend to be higher valued
